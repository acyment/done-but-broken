# CP06: Downgrade at period end

Plan downgrades never interrupt the paid period: they are scheduled and take effect at the next period renewal, with no proration lines.

## Scenarios

#### Scenario: Downgrade is scheduled
- **GIVEN** an active subscription with plan_changed change=downgrade
- **WHEN** the subscription view is queried before renewal
- **THEN** the current plan is unchanged and the pending plan is recorded

#### Scenario: Downgrade applies at renewal
- **GIVEN** a scheduled downgrade and a period_renewed event
- **WHEN** the subscription view is queried
- **THEN** the plan is the downgraded plan and no proration lines appear on later invoices

## Events introduced

- `period_renewed` with fields: `at`, `event_id`, `new_period_end`, `new_period_start`, `subscription_id`, `type`
- `plan_downgraded` with fields: `at`, `event_id`, `new_plan_id`, `new_plan_price_cents`, `subscription_id`, `type`
